Welcome to the Vestavia Hills Chamber of Commerce!

Vestavia Hills, the 3rd largest city in Jefferson County, is home to rolling green hills, blue ribbon schools, and a thriving business community. Incorporated in 1950, the city has grown to nearly 35,000 people. With a flourishing lineup of athletic activities, distinctive park system (including charming “pocket parks” utilizing small, once-unattended plots of city ground), and prosperous, well-educated citizenry, Vestavia Hills offers unparalleled opportunity to its business members. Proximity to Birmingham opens an additional array of cultural and networking events, as well as convenient access to international travel and import/export supply lines.
